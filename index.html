Why Your Instagram Isn't Growing (And It's Not Your Captions)

Good captions help, but they won't fix a broken strategy.

You can post the most relatable caption in the world and still get 40 views on a Reel. Why? Instagram looks at how fast people engage after you post, how long they watch your video, whether they save or share, and whether your account posts consistently enough to be recommended.

The accounts that grow fastest right now treat Instagram like a conversation, not a content dump. They show up regularly, respond to comments, create content for a specific person, and have a clear point of view.

Most creators skip the strategy part and jump straight to tactics. They copy trending sounds, post random captions, use 30 hashtags, and wonder why nothing sticks. That's not a content problem. It's a system problem.
